Other Ways to Add Wine

For an even faster experience, consider using one of these free helper applications.

Wonderful Wino Android Helper

If you have an Android phone with a camera, you can use the Vivino app to snap a picture of any wine bottle label. With a couple of clicks the Wonderful Wino Android Helper will then fill in your inventory with the wine’s details automatically. From the App, you can also set the vintage, quantity, and (optionally) the cost tier.

Wonderful Wino Chrome Extension

If you’re using the Chrome browser with the Wonderful Wino extension installed, adding a wine from Vivino is quick and easy. Just open the page for your wine on the Vivino website, then click the Wonderful Wino icon in your browser toolbar. The extension will automatically capture the wine’s details, letting you add it instantly. You can also specify the vintage, quantity, and (optionally) the cost tier directly from the extension.
