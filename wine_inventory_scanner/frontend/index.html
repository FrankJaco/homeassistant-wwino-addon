Maintenance Help

This section provides tools to manage your wine inventory database and its synchronization with Home Assistant.

  • Sync ToDo to DB: This button will synchronize your entire wine inventory from the Wonderful-Wino database to your Home Assistant To-Do list. It will first clear all existing items in the configured Home Assistant To-Do list and then re-add all wines from your inventory. Use this if your To-Do list in Home Assistant gets out of sync with your inventory.
  • Reset DB: WARNING: This action will permanently delete ALL your wine data from the Wonderful-Wino database. This cannot be undone. Use this only if you want to start your wine inventory from scratch.

For more detailed information on troubleshooting or advanced usage, please refer to the add-on documentation.
