How to Use Wonderful-Wino

Welcome to Wonderful-Wino, your personal wine inventory management tool!

  • Add New Wine: Use the form at the top to add wines to your inventory. Fill in the details and click "Add Wine".
  • Manage Inventory: Your wines will appear in the "Your Wine Inventory" table.
    • : Increase quantity.
    • : Decrease quantity.
    • : Delete the wine entry.
  • Search Vivino: Use the "Search Vivino" section to quickly look up wines on Vivino.com.
  • Sync Data: Share or merge your public inventory with another user! Enter their User ID and click "Sync Inventory". This will add their public wines to your inventory.
  • Your User ID: Your unique User ID is displayed at the top. Share this with others if you want to sync your public inventory with them.
