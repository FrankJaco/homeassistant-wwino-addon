Home Assistant Sync Status
Synchronize All Wines to Home Assistant To-Do

This action will force a full synchronization of all wines currently in your inventory database to your Home Assistant To-Do list (todo.my_wine).

Use this if you believe the lists are out of sync or if you've made manual changes to the database that need to be reflected in Home Assistant.

Important: This will attempt to add/update wines based on your database. If you have "orphaned" items in HA (not in your database), they will not be removed by this action. You may need to manually clear them if HA Sync is paused.

Reset Database Confirmation

WARNING: This action is irreversible!

Resetting the database will:

  • **Permanently delete ALL wine data** from your inventory.
  • Re-create an empty, fresh database.
  • This will also cause all your wines in Home Assistant's To-Do list to become "orphaned" (if you don't clear them manually first).
